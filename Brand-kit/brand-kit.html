USAGE

Patterns sit under type, never over it. Opacity never exceeds 12% on cream, 20% on ink. If a section feels busy, drop the pattern entirely — the grid is enough rhythm.

PAIRINGS

A on teal for editorial, B on cream for merch + shipping tape + tote bags, C behind hero lines only — always soft, always off-centre.

SPOT USE

The ✦ star motif pulled from the wordmark can be cropped into campaign frames, Instagram story backgrounds, and full-bleed on OOH placements.
